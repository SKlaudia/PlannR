About

PlannR is an app designed to help students with their planning, timetabling, and overall work ethic. Here's how it works:

The problem:

Students no matter at which educational level they are at go through too much school work which leads to pressure in meeting deadlines and allocating enough revision time.

The Solution

PlannR will allow students at all educational levels to stay organised and informed about any important dates and to doâ€™s. This is accomplished by giving notifications in advance as well as providing their class timetable with revision slots in between. PlannR will also allow students to make their own revision timetable for holidays or half-terms with a system that gives students stars that they earn whilst doing their set tasks or revision.
